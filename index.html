How to Play

Game Objective

Use the directional keys to move your character and collect all the targets to complete each level.

Controls

  • Pause: Space bar or ESC key
  • Fullscreen: F11 key

Tips

  • Take your time - there's no time pressure
  • Use the settings to adjust movement speed
  • Enable input buffering if controls feel too sensitive
  • You can't lose - focus on learning and improving
